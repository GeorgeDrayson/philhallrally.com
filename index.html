2020 Calendar Coming Soon

In the meantime check out some of the incredible rallies we did in 2019

Race 1: Rally Sweden

Based in Torsby, Sweden | 14-17 February 2019

Very fast Snow/Ice stages which cross into Norway
Spectacular special stage in the centre of Karlstad.
Studded tyres give incredible grip on the sheet ice!

Race 2: Tour de Corse

Based in Bastia, Corsica | 28-31 March 2019

Twisty, narrow, legendary tarmac stages.
Demands excellent pacenotes and optimum tyre choices.
One of the most famous rallies in the world!

Race 3: Rally Italia Sardegna

Based in Alghero, Sardinia | 13-16 June 2019

Rough, challenging, car destroying event.
Focus on not damaging the car and avoiding punctures.
A clean line and just keeping going will pay dividends.

Race 4: Rally Finland

Based in Jyvaskyla, Finland | 1-4 August 2019

The fastest event on the calendar: The gravel Grand Prix!
Cars literally fly as they take off over the jumps and crests on the high speed gravel roads.
Bravery is rewarded, and mistakes can be heavily punished by close trees.

Race 5: Wales Rally GB

Based in North Wales, UK | 3-6 October 2019

Our home event, known for fog, rain, and very difficult conditions.
Carrying speed in low visibility is what will win you this event,
and understanding the grip levels in the muddy forests.
